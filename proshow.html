Aditi Singh Sharma

Aditi Singh Sharma is an Indian playback singer. She made her playback singing debut in Bollywood with Anurag Kashyap's Dev.D. Sharma is known for her work in movies like Dev.D, No One Killed, Dhoom 3 and 2 States

Nalayak

Naalayak is a 4 piece indie rock band from Chandigarh. Known for their explosive live acts and pumping music. They are now considered to be the genre defining new era of indie rock.
